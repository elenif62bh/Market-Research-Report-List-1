<p><p>The veterinary endoscopy system market is categorized into flexible endoscopes, rigid endoscopes, and other endoscopes. Flexible endoscopes are lightweight, allowing for maneuverability in various anatomical structures, making them ideal for gastrointestinal and respiratory examinations. Rigid endoscopes provide stability and are often used for surgical procedures or detailed interior examinations, typically in larger animals. Other endoscopes encompass specialized instruments like video endoscopes and transendoscopic tools, enhancing diagnostic capabilities across different veterinary disciplines and procedures.</p></p>
