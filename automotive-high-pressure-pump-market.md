<p><strong>The Automotive High-Pressure Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plunger High-Pressure Pump</li><li>Port and Helix High-Pressure Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive high-pressure pump market is primarily categorized into two types: plunger high-pressure pumps and port and helix high-pressure pumps. Plunger high-pressure pumps utilize a plunger mechanism to create the required pressure for fuel injection, offering high efficiency and reliability. In contrast, port and helix high-pressure pumps employ a helical groove mechanism for fluid movement, optimizing fuel delivery in various engine conditions. Both types play a crucial role in modern fuel systems, enhancing engine performance and efficiency.</p></p>
