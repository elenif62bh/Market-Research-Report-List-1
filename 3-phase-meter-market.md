<p><ul><li>Half Hourly or Non-Half Hourly Meter</li><li>Automated Meter Reading (AMR)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 3-Phase Meter market features three main types: Half Hourly (HH) meters, Non-Half Hourly (NHH) meters, and Automated Meter Reading (AMR) systems. Half Hourly meters capture energy consumption in 30-minute intervals, enabling precise billing and demand management. Non-Half Hourly meters record usage periodically, typically leading to less frequent billing cycles. Automated Meter Reading systems facilitate remote data collection for both HH and NHH meters, enhancing accuracy, efficiency, and real-time monitoring of energy usage for consumers and utilities alike.</p></p>
