<p><strong>The Agricultural Horizontal Vortex Water Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Closed Vortex Pump</li><li>Open Vortex Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Agricultural Horizontal Vortex Water Pump market includes two primary types: closed vortex pumps and open vortex pumps. Closed vortex pumps feature a fully enclosed impeller design, providing efficient hydraulic performance and reducing turbulence, making them suitable for applications requiring high flow rates and minimal debris handling. In contrast, open vortex pumps incorporate a more open impeller structure that allows for handling larger solids, making them ideal for agricultural irrigation and wastewater applications where clogging is a concern.</p></p>
