<p><p>The Continuous Extrusion Blow Molding Machines market includes two primary types: Shuttle Machines and Rotary Wheel Machines. Shuttle Machines utilize a linear movement system where molds open and close in a shuttle-style motion, allowing for efficient production of various container shapes. In contrast, Rotary Wheel Machines employ a rotating system where molds are positioned around a wheel, enabling continuous operation and higher output rates. Both types cater to a range of applications, including packaging and industrial uses, enhancing productivity and flexibility.</p></p>
