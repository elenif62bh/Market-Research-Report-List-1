<p><ul><li>Pneumatic Controlled Valves</li><li>Electric Controlled Valves</li><li>Hydraulic Controlled Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The controlled valves market encompasses three primary types: pneumatic, electric, and hydraulic controlled valves. Pneumatic controlled valves utilize compressed air to regulate flow, making them ideal for systems requiring quick response. Electric controlled valves operate using electrical signals, offering precise control and integration with automated systems. Hydraulic controlled valves function through pressurized fluid, suited for high-force applications. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, impacting various sectors such as manufacturing, oil and gas, and water management.</p></p>
