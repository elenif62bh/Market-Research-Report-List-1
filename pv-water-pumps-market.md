<p><strong>The PV Water Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Suction</li><li>Submersible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PV water pumps market primarily comprises two types: surface suction and submersible pumps. Surface suction pumps are installed above ground and draw water from a source using suction, making them suitable for shallow water bodies. In contrast, submersible pumps are designed to operate underwater, directly pumping water to the surface from greater depths. Each type has distinct applications, with surface pumps often favored for simplicity and cost-effectiveness, while submersibles are utilized for deeper or more challenging water extraction conditions.</p></p>
