<p><p>The polyurethane O-ring seals market is categorized into non-reinforced seals and fabric-reinforced seals. Non-reinforced seals are made entirely of polyurethane, offering flexibility and chemical resistance, suitable for less demanding applications. In contrast, fabric-reinforced seals incorporate layers of fabric to enhance strength, durability, and performance under high pressure and extreme conditions. Fabric-reinforced seals are ideal for heavy-duty applications, such as automotive and industrial systems, where added resilience is crucial for longevity and reliability.</p></p>
