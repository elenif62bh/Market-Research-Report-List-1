<p><p>Baggage carousel markets are categorized into single-level and multi-level systems. Single-level carousels feature a single, circular rotating band where all passengers collect their luggage, optimizing space in smaller airports. In contrast, multi-level carousels consist of stacked systems that can accommodate more bags, benefiting larger airports with higher passenger traffic. Both designs aim to enhance efficiency, minimize wait times, and improve passenger experience by ensuring timely baggage delivery while addressing the logistical needs of different airport sizes.</p></p>
